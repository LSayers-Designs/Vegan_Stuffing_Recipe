Level 1: Vegan Gluten Free Stuffing

Ingredients

  • 4C. Gluten Free Stuffing Mix
  • 2C. Vegetable Broth
  • 1/2 C. Carrots
  • 1/2 C. Celery
  • 1/4 C. Cranberries
  • 1/4 C. small onion
  • 2 Tbs. Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp. sage
  • 1 tsp. thyme
  • 1 tsp. parsley
  • 1 tsp. paprika
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • Salt and Pepper

Reccomended Materials

  • Stove/Cook Top
  • Oven
  • Measuring cup
  • 3 qt sauce pan or Dutch Oven
  • Wooden stiring spoon
  • 11" x 8" cutting board
  • Chefs Knife
  • 5-6 prep bowls
  • Baking/Serving Dish (in lieu of a dutch oven)

FOOD PREP AND DIRECTIONS

To begin, dice carrots, celery, onion and parsley and set aside in prep individual prep bowls.

In a separate prep bowl measure out the dried herbs and spices. If you are using preseasoned stuffing, then omit the step completely and only measure out the stuffing mix and set it aside.

In a medium sauce pan (or Dutch Oven)heat the olive oil and add carrots, celery and onion and saute until fragrant. Then add herbs and spices mix along with vegetable broth and bring to a light boil.

Incorporate stuffing to the sauce pan and ensure the broth is fully absorbed and remove from heat. If the stuffing mixture is too dry add vegetable stock 1/4 cup at a time until lightly moist but not soaking wet.

Transfer mixture into a baking dish if not using a dutch oven, and place in the oven uncovered for 15 minutes or until the top begins to crisp. Remove from oven allow to set for five (5) minutes.

Lastly, sprinkle with fresh oregano and serve.
